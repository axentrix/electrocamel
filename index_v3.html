IN THE HEART OF VIENNA

Our food stand is located in the center of Vienna on Mariahilfer str. 47. If you need a shopping break or you’re just passing by, visit us for a quick bite. We guarantee delicious food with vegetarian, vegan and gluten free options, good music and good mood. Come on over and try our fresh salads, loaded pitas and smooooooth hummus using only the freshest ingredients.

Our Dishes

Camel Rocket Falafel (V)

Pita with falafel, hummus, green Therbs, tomatoes, cucumber, red cabbage, pickled red onions, olive oil and lemon. Topped with tahini.

Eggplant Sunshine

Hummus in a Pita, baked eggplant, boiled egg, mint, parsley, tomatoes, cucumber, potato, olive oil and lemon, pickled red onions, caramelized onions, topped with tahini and spicy red chili paste.

Mushroom Rainbow Mix (V)

Hummus in a Pita, mushrooms a la Plancha, tomatoes, cucumber, olive oil and lemon pickled onions and green herbs. Topped with tahini / green tahini and amba yogurt.

Electro Hummus (V)

Freshly made on the spot, with olive oil, sweet paprika, parsley, boiled egg. Served with a pita.

Electro Hummus & mushrooms (V)

Same great Hummus with sauteed mushrooms a la Plancha, cumin and baharat, olive oil, parsley. Served with a pita.

Market Salad (V) (GF)

Baked potatoes and eggplants, soft-boiled egg, red onions, rocket leaves, fresh herbs, olive oil, lemon juice, topped with beet tahini and amba seasoned yogurt.

Electro Camel Salad (V) (GF)

Cooked lentils and chickpeas, pickled red onions, cherry tomatoes, celery, rocket leaves, fresh herbs, olive oil, lemon juice and beet tahini.

Malabi (V)

Mediterranean coconut and almond cream, with rose water and pomegranate, pistachios and shredded coconut.

Chocolate bomb

Rich chocolate ganache topped with salted cocoa crumble and halva strings.
